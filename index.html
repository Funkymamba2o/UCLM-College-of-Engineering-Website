Mission, Vision, & Core Values

MISSION

The mission of the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue College of Engineering is to provide quality education and produce competent and socially responsible engineers who will contribute to the sustainable development of the region and the country as a whole.

VISION

The University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue College of Engineering aims to be a leading engineering institution that fosters innovation and excellence in education, research, and community engagement.

CORE VALUES

Excellence- We strive for academic and professional excellence in all our endeavors.
Integrity - We uphold honesty, accountability, and transparency in all our actions.
Innovation - We encourage creativity, critical thinking, and a culture of continuous learning and improvement.
