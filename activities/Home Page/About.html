ABOUT US

Budget meal Musical Instruments and Accessories!

JCraft's goal is give highly playable instrument to the players right out of the box. To make this possible, we require all our dealers to setup each and every guitar before shipping to the customer. Each guitar's truss rod, nut action, saddle height are adjusted to lower the action to a playable quality. Because of this, we have gained a lot of positive feedback from customers.


JCraft is distributed in Asia-Pacific by Micasa Trading Hong Kong, and in the Philippines at Jolly Music. JCraft guitars is currently sold in Lazada and Shopee by Jolly Music.
